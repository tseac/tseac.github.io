Our Bylaws:

ARTICLE I. INTRODUCTION

The official name of this club shall be the Space Exploration Advocacy Club. Hereafter referred to as the/this club. The official purpose of this club is to promote the exploration of outer space. The mission of this club is to advocate for space exploration, to explore any and every world we come across, to foster a new, private sector space race, on the scale of such seen between the Americans and the Soviets, in which we landed on the moon, to advocate, educate, and explore the awesome possibilities of human space exploration, and to attenuate criticism of and foster excitement for space exploration. We are those who see the reasons behind “why," and choose to answer, “why not.” UPDATED 10/19/18-VP E MONTI WOOLF(edited)

ARTICLE II. MEMBERSHIP

Potential members must contact a member of the club to request membership. New members must be approved by the President of the club. Until a dedicated website is created, all members must have a Quora account in order to be a member, and a Discord account in order to use this channel. All members will be awarded authorship on the blog. In addition to membership, members can be bestowed honorary knighthood by the President. This does not give them any special rank or duty in the club. It simply awards them the honorific of Sir. Members may give up their membership at any time. Such members will be subsequently subject to loss of authorship, along with being removed from the Discord

ARTICLE III. MEETING LOCATION

This club will be entirely online. This club will have no official physical presence. Until the creation of a dedicated website, the blog along with this Discord Server will serve as headquarters of the club.

Quora blog, or to a channel on this Discord server. Posts will be discussed in comments on Quora with upvotes given as a sign of approval. Here, it will be discussed in subsequent messages. The blog will be moderated by the President, Vice-President, and Secretary. Posts will be subject to removal by the President. The Discord server will be moderated by the Owner, President, Vice President, and Secretary. If a conclusion is reached to perform certain actions, the President, and secretary will be responsible for facilitating it. Posts will be directed to certain executives and other members of club if it falls under their area of expertise.

ARTICLE V. MEETING TIMES

Due to the nature of this club, there will be no set meeting times.

ARTICLE VI. EXECUTIVES

Executive officers will be nominated by the President, except The President, who will be whoever was the previous President’s vice-president. Each officer will have a set category office, which will be customizable by the officer who holds the position.

Even if the president does not wish for his/her vice-president to succeed him/her, the vice-president will still do so.

Anyone who is nominated to an executive position due to a presidential nomination or a vice-presidential succession to President is allowed to decline the nomination.

In the event the vice-president chooses to decline his/her automatic succession, leaving the club with no acting President, the secretary will nominate the new president.

The nomination must be confirmed by the nominee and vice-president.

The president shall be the chief executive of the club.

The president as of writing is Dawn Amber, and the associated owner of that account.

The vice-president shall serve as the assistant executive officer. They are to be promoted to President upon the President’s leave of the club due to death, or retirement.

The vice-president as of writing is E. Monti Woolf and the associated owner of that account.

The secretary shall be responsible for moderating the discussions and facilitating this club’s activities.

The secretary as of writing is Ciaran Love and the associated owner of that account.

The treasurer shall be responsible for handling the club’s financials. As no such such responsibilities exist yet, this role is only honorary. The treasurer as of writing is Zachary Smith and the associated owner of that account.

The historian shall be responsible for maintaining records of the club. The historian as of writing is Charlotte Grey and the associated owner of that account.

Certain members of the club may fulfill specific duties under the administration of an executive, thus forming departments, with the executive as chief officer.

ARTICLE VII. PROCEDURE FOR AMENDMENTS

(This article has been repealed of 10/20/18) This charter is subject to change at the President’s discretion

AMENDMENT 1-PROCEDURE FOR FUTURE AMENDMENTS
  1. The title must be in the format:

    2. Bylaws: Proposed Amendment #x: [Amendment Title]

  2. The body will suggest an amendment, with any descriptions shown in Italics. Descriptions will be omitted in the case that the amendment is passed.
  3. Any amendment must be officially approved by a majority of the officers, using either: Discord poll OR comment poll through Quora. This amendment is NOT subject to a vote, due to its nature. HOWEVER, this amendment must be approved by the President, Dawn Amber, before any more amendments can be officiated.
  4. This amendment will also remove ARTICLE VII from the bylaws of this club.

AMENDMENT 2-DELETION OF HEAD OF MARKETING ROLE
  1. The purpose of this amendment is to destroy the role of Head of Marketing and spread the included duties to the other 5 Executive officials.
  2. As of the ratification of this amendment, the role of Head of Marketing will be deleted from the Discord server, the website, and the official bylaws.
  3. The role of Head of Marketing was to spread the word about this club. This role will be taken and made a requirement of all officers. In other words, all officers will now have a responsibility to promote the club.
